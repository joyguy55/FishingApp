Hunting for Golden Trout in the Fitzpatrick Wilderness

The Fitzpatrick Wildernes resides in the Northern one-third of the Wyoming mountain Wind-Rivers eastern mountain range. This part of the Wind Rivers is within the Shoshone National Forest. The roadless area of the Wind River Indian Reservation is in the center.
